What is UltraCam

UltraCam is a mod Developed By MaxLastBreath alongside NX-Optimizer. The goal of the mod is to enchance the graphics of the game and allow for Dynamic Gamespeed at over 60+ FPS.

What UltraCam Offers
  • Smoother Gameplay: Achieve smooth dynamic 60+ FPS gameplay on both the Nintendo Switch and Switch emulators, ensuring an immersive and responsive experience.
  • Tweaked Physics: Physics have been optimized to run effectively at higher frame rates.
  • Higher Graphic Fidelity: Allows you to Increase the Internal resolution and offers Quality Improvements. Up to 8K Resolution and 8K Shadow Resolution.
  • Advanced Free Camera: Allows you to capture moments of your game with ease. Offering Free Camera, Sequencer, Keyframes and Teleportation.
  • Customizable Settings: Tailor UltraCam to your preferences with user-friendly customization options for a personalized experience.
Why Choose UltraCam?
  • Reliability and Performance: A lightweight code injection mod designed for optimal performance without compromising quality.
  • Unique Features: Oferring Command Console, Actor Spawner, Teleportation, Free Camera and Lots more.
  • Constant Development: TOTK UltraCam is being developed everyday by MaxLastBreath.
