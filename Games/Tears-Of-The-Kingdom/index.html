The Legend of Zelda: Tears Of The Kingdom

UltraCam: UltraCam is a mod developed by MaxLastBreath that enhances the game's performance by enabling smoother framerates and higher resolutions within its internal engine. It also introduces a drone-like camera view (FreeCam) for users to explore Photo or Video creation at their leisure. The Early Access version includes a comprehensive in-game menu, a First-Person Mode, and a reshade-like menu with advanced options to further enhance your graphics experience.

Supported Features

  • Dynamic FramerateAllows the game to run at over 30 FPS up to 240FPS without speeding up or slowing down.
  • Internal ResolutionChanges the rendering resolution on an engine level. Allowing the game to run at 1440P, 4K, 8K or Higher.
  • Shadow ResolutionChanges the in-game shadow resolution allowing the game to render shadows at higher than native resolution, allowing shadows such as 1024x1024, 2048x2048, 4096x4096 and 8192x8192.
  • Hide Game UIAllows you to hide all of the In-Game UI, in order to take better Screenshots or make the game more immersive..
  • Camera SpeedAllows you to customize your camera speed to your desire.
  • ConfigurationSupports Configuration with an injected config file.
  • UltraCam (FreeCam)Supports FreeCam, allowing you to get a drone-like view of the world.
  • Sequencer (FreeCam)Allows you to capture keyframes and interpolate between them, making it easier to create cinematic video shots.
  • BenchmarkSupports Benchmarks, allowing you to test your framerate or Switch/PC Stability.
  • First PersonTransforms the game in First Person Mode.
  • Time ControlAllows you to change the time of day during FreeCam. Also Allows you to change the speed of day.
  • Weather ControlControl the Weather Freely During FreeCam.
  • Overlay MenuSupports UltraCam Overlay Menu.
  • In-Game CheatsSupports UltraCam In-Game Cheats.
  • Actor SpawnerSupports UltraCam Actor Spawner.
  • Debug ConsoleSupports UltraCam Console.
  • Effects OverlaySupports UltraCam Console.
