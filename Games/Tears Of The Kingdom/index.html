The Legend of Zelda:

Tears Of The Kingdom

UltraCam: UltraCam is a mod developed by MaxLastBreath that enhances the game's performance by enabling framerates over 60 FPS and higher resolutions within its internal engine. It also introduces a drone-like camera view (FreeCam) for users to explore Photo or Video creation at their leisure. The Early Access version includes a comprehensive in-game menu, a First-Person Mode and a reshade-like menu with advanced options to further enhance your gameplay experience.
