Switch Games Mod Manager

NX-Optimizer (Known As TOTK-Optimizer) is a Mod Manager for UltraCam, a code injection mod for the following Games:

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, Breath Of The Wild, Echoes Of Wisdom, Link's Awakening and More

Featuring Game Optimization, Dynamic Framerate, 60+ FPS, Higher Resolutions, Higher Shadow Resolution, Free Camera, Cheats and More...
